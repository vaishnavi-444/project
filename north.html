UNION TERRITORIES (NORTH)
A union territory is a type of administrative division in the Republic of India. Unlike the states of India, which have their own governments, union territories are federal territories governed, in part or in whole, by the Union Government of India. Geographical Features
North India States are landlocked and are surrounded by some of the most striking geographical features. From deserts to mountains, there is so much that a tourist can see in the northern part of India. Here are the details about the geography of the northern states of India. Roughly, the north Indian states are guarded by the mighty Himalayas in the north and the Indo-Gangetic plains towards the south and the east. The dominant geographical feature towards the west of the northern states in India is the arid Indian desert - the Thar. The Himalayas make up a natural border demarcating India from the Tibetian Plateau and the Karakorams of Central Asia.
